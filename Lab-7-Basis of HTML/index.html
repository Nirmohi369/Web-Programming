Rajasthan

Rajasthan(Land of Kings) is a place of camels plodding over soft sand dunes of the Thar Desert. It is a state of men with proud mustaches and women with twinkling anklets in colorful swirling ghagras. Rajasthan is home of the valiant Rajputs known for their bravery and chivalry.

I visited Rajasthan during my high school. It was fun visiting all places with friends. Lakes and all historic forts were amazing, and the architectural resaons of how they were build to protect King and people from enemies. Best time to visit Rajasathan is October to November, as the days are not too hot and nights are not too cold during that period. Below are the list of places I liked the most during my visit.

  1. Fateh Sagar Lake
  2. Kumbhalgarh Fort
  3. Ranthambore National Park
  4. Jaisalmer Fort
  5. Jal Mahal

Toronto

Toronto is Canada's largest city and a melting pot of cultures, flavors, and attractions. Visitors from around the world flock to this part of Canada to see one-of-a-kind landmarks, meander bustling streets, and explore remarkable museums. Toronto has a little something for every kind of visitor.

My visit to Canada was in summer 2023 with my parents. Our first stop was Niagara Falls, as we drove from New Jersey. Niagara Falls has its own beauty. For the first time in my life, I saw a Rainbow form in Water due to mist and sunlight. Also, CN Tower is must go place while visiting Toronto.It is the tallet building in Canada, also the city view from the glass walls is outstanding. Below are the list of places I liked the most during my visit.

  1. Niagara Falls
  2. CN Tower
  3. Royal Ontario Museum
  4. Nathan Philips Square
  5. Toronto Islands

Leh Ladakh

Leh Ladakh is a must-visit destination for its awe-inspiring landscapes, adventure opportunities, unique culture, and spiritual atmosphere. Leh Ladakh is known for its rich Tibetan and Buddhist culture. The clear skies and minimal light pollution in Leh Ladakh make it an excellent location for stargazing and astrophotography.

Visiting Leh Ladakh was a most awaited trip for my dad. He always wanted to do thrilling road trip along challenging mountain passes and high altitudes. Nubra Valley has an mixture of mountain, river and dessert all at one place. We also visited Pangong lake, situated between mountains and is 135 kms long, the water is crystal clear, that stones are visible from a height.   1. Pangong Lake
  2. Zanskar Valley
  3. Nubra Valley
  4. Spituk Gompa
  5. Thiksey Monastery

Washington DC

Washington, D.C. is the capital of the United States and serves as the seat of the federal government. Known for its iconic landmarks like the White House, the Washington Monument, and the Capitol Building, the city is rich in history, politics, and cultural significance.

You can explore the rich history and culture of the United States by touring world-famous monuments and museums, such as the National Mall, Smithsonian museums, and the Lincoln Memorial. You'll also witness the political heart of the nation with visits to the Capitol, the Supreme Court, and the White House, providing an opportunity to gain insight into the workings of the U.S. government. Additionally, the city offers a vibrant food scene, diverse neighborhoods, and a welcoming atmosphere that make it a must-visit destination for travelers. Below are the list of places I liked the most during my visit.

  1. Lincoln Memorial
  2. National Air and Space Museum
  3. US Capitol Building
  4. Washington Monument
  5. National Mall
