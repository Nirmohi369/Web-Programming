Kumbhalgarh

10/11/2015 9:30 PM

Today, I had the privilege of exploring the historic and awe-inspiring Kumbhalgarh Fort in Rajasthan, India. This magnificent fort, often referred to as the "Great Wall of India," is perched atop the Aravalli Hills, surrounded by a vast expanse of lush green forests.

Rajasthan

Pangong Lake

06/20/2022 7:45 PM

My journey to Pangong Lake began with a scenic drive through the dramatic and rugged terrain of Ladakh. As I approached the lake, the first glimpse of its azure waters amidst the barren, rocky landscape took my breath away.

I stood at the shore, gazing at the crystal-clear waters that seemed to change color as the sun moved across the sky. The various shades of blue, from deep navy to turquoise, were a testament to the lake's pristine environment. I decided to take a leisurely walk along the lake's shore, which allowed me to soak in the breathtaking views and appreciate the quietude of this remote location. The chilly mountain breeze, the calls of migratory birds, and the solitude of the surroundings made me feel like I was in a different world.

Leh Ladakh

Niagra Falls

08/17/2023 11:15 PM

As I approached Niagara Falls, the thundering sound of rushing water could be heard from a distance, building anticipation with every step. The falls, straddling the border between the United States and Canada, offer various vantage points to appreciate their majesty.

The view from the Canadian side of the falls is nothing short of spectacular, offering a breathtaking panorama that left an indelible impression on me. As I arrived at Niagara Falls in Ontario, Canada, I was immediately struck by the lush and well-maintained Queen Victoria Park, which lines the Niagara River and provides an excellent vantage point for admiring the falls. The park is meticulously landscaped and serves as the perfect gateway to this magnificent natural wonder.

Toronto

National Air and Space Museum

07/23/2023 8:00 PM

Today, I explored the National Air and Space Museum in Washington, D.C., marveling at the pioneering spirit of human flight and the incredible spacecraft that carried us beyond our atmosphere. The museum's collection, from the Wright brothers' plane to the Apollo 11 module, left me inspired and in awe of our journey into the skies and beyond. I also watched a documentary "Dark Universe", which explores the mysteries of dark matter and dark energy, the invisible forces that shape the universe.

Washington DC
